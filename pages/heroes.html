Hero Roles

Understanding The Roles

Each role brings unique strengths to your team composition.

⚔️

Assault

Frontline damage dealers who engage enemies and secure eliminations. High damage output with moderate survivability.

🛡️

Tank

Heavily armored protectors who absorb damage and create space for their team. Essential for controlling objectives.

⚡

Duelist

High mobility assassins who flank enemies and eliminate key targets. Require precision and positioning mastery.

💚

Support

Essential healers and buffers who keep teammates alive and enhance their capabilities in battle.
