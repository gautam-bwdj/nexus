Core Mechanics

How To Play

Master these fundamental mechanics to excel in combat.

01

Movement & Positioning

Fluid movement system with advanced parkour mechanics. Wall-running, sliding, and double jumps give you tactical advantages. Position yourself strategically to control the battlefield.

  • Advanced parkour system
  • Wall-running and climbing
  • Tactical sliding and rolls
  • Hero-specific mobility abilities
02

Ability System

Each hero has 3 unique abilities plus an ultimate. Abilities have cooldowns and require strategic timing. Combine abilities with teammates for devastating combos.

  • 3 active abilities per hero
  • Ultimate ability charged by combat
  • Cooldown management
  • Ability combos and synergies
03

Team Composition

Build balanced teams with tanks, damage dealers, and support heroes. Communication and coordination are key to victory. Adapt your composition to counter enemies.

  • 6v6 team-based combat
  • Role synergies and counters
  • Strategic hero switching
  • Voice and text communication
04

Objective Control

Different game modes require different strategies. Control key points, escort payloads, or capture zones. Team coordination and map awareness determine victory.

  • Multiple objective types
  • Dynamic spawn points
  • Map control strategies
  • Time-based challenges
Game Modes

Choose Your Battle

Multiple modes to test your skills and strategies.

🎯

Control Point

Capture and hold strategic points across the map. Teams fight for dominance, earning points over time. First to 100% wins.

6v6 Teams 15-20 min
🚛

Payload Escort

Push the payload to its destination while the enemy team tries to stop you. Attackers must maintain presence near the payload.

6v6 Teams 12-18 min
⚔️

Team Deathmatch

Pure combat focused mode. First team to reach 50 eliminations wins. Respawns are instant. Perfect for warming up and practicing.

6v6 Teams 8-12 min
👑

King of the Hill

Single control point that moves after each round. Best of 3 rounds wins the match. Intense focused combat.

6v6 Teams 10-15 min
🏆

Ranked Competitive

Climb the competitive ladder through skill-based matchmaking. Earn seasonal rewards and exclusive cosmetics based on your rank.

6v6 Teams 20-30 min
🎪

Special Events

Pro Tips

Master The Game

💡

Communication Wins Games

Use voice chat and ping system to coordinate with your team. Call out enemy positions and ultimate abilities.

💡

Learn Hero Counters

Every hero has strengths and weaknesses. Switch heroes mid-match to counter the enemy team composition.

💡

Objective First

Eliminations don't win games - objectives do. Always prioritize the objective over individual kills.

💡

Ultimate Economy

Coordinate ultimate abilities with your team. Combining ultimates can win team fights and secure objectives.
