Core Mechanics

How To Play

Master these fundamental mechanics to excel in combat.

01

Movement & Positioning

Fluid movement system with advanced parkour mechanics. Wall-running, sliding, and double jumps give you tactical advantages. Position yourself strategically to control the battlefield.

  • Advanced parkour system
  • Wall-running and climbing
  • Tactical sliding and rolls
  • Hero-specific mobility abilities
02

Ability System

Each hero has 3 unique abilities plus an ultimate. Abilities have cooldowns and require strategic timing. Combine abilities with teammates for devastating combos.

  • 3 active abilities per hero
  • Ultimate ability charged by combat
  • Cooldown management
  • Ability combos and synergies
03

Team Composition

Build balanced teams with tanks, damage dealers, and support heroes. Communication and coordination are key to victory. Adapt your composition to counter enemies.

  • 6v6 team-based combat
  • Role synergies and counters
  • Strategic hero switching
  • Voice and text communication
04

Objective Control

Different game modes require different strategies. Control key points, escort payloads, or capture zones. Team coordination and map awareness determine victory.

  • Multiple objective types
  • Dynamic spawn points
  • Map control strategies
  • Time-based challenges
